This report aims to present information on the potential benefits and challenges of telemedicine in disaster response scenarios. Telemedicine is a remote healthcare solution that allows healthcare providers to diagnose and treat patients remotely through video conferencing and other remote monitoring devices. In the event of a disaster, telemedicine can play a crucial role in providing quick and efficient medical care to people in need. This report will present the benefits and challenges of telemedicine in disaster response scenarios.
